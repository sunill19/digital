About Us

Digital India Seva" refers to the various digital platforms and initiatives launched by the Government of India as part of its larger "Digital India" campaign. "Digital India" is an ambitious initiative launched in 2015 with the vision to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. The initiative aims to leverage digital technologies to bridge the digital divide, promote inclusive growth, and empower citizens through various digital services and infrastructure. "Digital India Seva" specifically focuses on providing various online services and support to citizens through digital platforms. These services cover a wide range of areas including education, healthcare, governance, financial services, agriculture, and more. The goal is to make government services more accessible, efficient, and transparent through the use of digital technologies.
